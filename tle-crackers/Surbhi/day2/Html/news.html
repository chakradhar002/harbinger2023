Women's Cricket News

South Africa captain Sune Luus won the toss and opted to bowl in the women's T20I tri-series opener against India in East London. In the absence of Harmanpreet Kaur, who was unwell, India were led by Smriti Mandhana.

Shubhman gill on Wednesday became the eighth double centurion in ODI cricket as he scored 208 against New Zealand in Hyderabad. The 23-year-old Gill owned the stage and became the youngest batter to score a double hundred in ODI history.
