PHOTOSHOOT EFFECT

A photograph is a gift of science which is always close to heart. Because it contains memories of people near &far. Every photo simple or complex has a story to stay after Becomes fresh in our mind. It is a creation very small but is full of details, movement &emotions of a person.

 Photographs are taken from a gadget known as a camera, earlier there were big box shaped camera which had a shutter & could only capture black & white photos. But as time passed by were developments in the camera settings & the quality of photograph as well. Nowadays photographs clicked from high definition camera are like reality, so beautiful & realistic. People nowadays hire photographers before some Special event like pre-wedding photo shoot, on the day of the wedding, birthdays get together to capture the special moments for a life time.
