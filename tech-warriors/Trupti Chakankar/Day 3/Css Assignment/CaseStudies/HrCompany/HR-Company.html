HR For Small Bussiness

Human resources (HR) is the set of people who make up the workforce of an organization, business sector, industry, or economy. A narrower concept is human capital, the knowledge and skills which the individuals command. Similar terms include manpower, labor, personnel, associates or simply: people. The Human Resources department (HR department) of an organization performs human resource management,overseeing various aspects of employment, such as compliance with labor law and employment standards, interviewing and selection.
